About Us

Welcome to Polio contest

Welcome to the Rotary Club Children’s Polio Competition!

Our mission is to engage everyone in the fight against polio through creativity, education, and community involvement. This competition encourages parents/guardians to explore the importance of vaccination and the global efforts to eradicate polio.

Faq’s

Frequntly Ask Questions

  • Q. How can I enter my baby in the competition?

    To enter, visit our official website and fill out the online registration form. You’ll need to upload a recent photo of your baby and provide some basic information, such as their age and your contact details. Make sure to submit your entry before the deadline!

  • Q. Are there any age restrictions for participants?

    Yes, participants must be between 0 to 3 years old at the time of entry. Ensure that your baby's age falls within this range to qualify for the competition.

  • Q. How is the winner chosen?

    Winners are determined through a combination of public voting and a panel of judges. Public votes account for a percentage of the final score, while the judges evaluate based on criteria such as cuteness, personality, and creativity in the submitted photo.
